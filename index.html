Who We Are

A Staffing Partner Built for Modern Businesses

UrbanStaff Partners is a modern recruitment and staffing firm helping businesses across India streamline their hiring and workforce acquisition. We work with startups, growing companies, BPOs, retail businesses, and mid-sized organizations.

Our work is straightforward: understand what you need, find people who fit, coordinate effectively, and support you through to successful joining. No fluff, no delays.

What Makes Our Approach Different

We focus on structured hiring, not just resume forwarding. Every profile we share is evaluated against your specific requirement before it reaches your desk.

  • Pre-screened candidates matched to your job description
  • Dedicated point of contact for each requirement
  • Clear timelines and regular updates throughout the process
  • Support across contract, full-time, and bulk hiring models
  • Pan-India network across metros and Tier-2 cities
  • No hidden charges or misleading placement guarantees
